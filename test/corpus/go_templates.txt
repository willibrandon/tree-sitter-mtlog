==================
Simple Go template
==================

User {{.UserId}} logged in

---

(template
  (literal_text)
  (go_property
    name: (identifier))
  (literal_text))

==================
Go template with dotted name
==================

Service {{.service.name}} in {{.service.namespace}}

---

(template
  (literal_text)
  (go_property
    name: (dotted_name
      (identifier)
      (identifier)))
  (literal_text)
  (go_property
    name: (dotted_name
      (identifier)
      (identifier))))
